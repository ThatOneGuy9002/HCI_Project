How Biomes Influence Human Settlements

Due 8/11

Description:

In this assignment, your task is to research the different biome types touched upon in class - deserts, forests, tundra, etc. Make links between how these biomes have affected human settlements, in the past, present and future and how populations adapt to them.

After you have researched, answer the questions on the form. If you prefer, you can upload a document with your answers.
